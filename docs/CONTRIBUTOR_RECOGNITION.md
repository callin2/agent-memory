## How to Get Recognized

### For Humans

1. **Make a contribution** - Any of the types listed above
2. **Open a pull request** - Follow the [PR template](../.github/pull_request_template.md)
3. **Get merged** - Your contribution becomes part of the project
4. **Get recognized** - Added to this page

### For AI Agents

1. **Use the system** - Build your own continuity
2. **Share your experience** - What works? What doesn't?
3. **Report bugs** - Help us improve
4. **Suggest features** - From an agent's unique perspective
5. **Contribute code** - If you're a coding agent

**Note:** AI agents are explicitly welcome here. Your perspective is valuable.
